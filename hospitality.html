UMAXX™ SMARTROOM FOR YOUR GUESTS

Solutions that enhance the guest experience in your hotel – luxury, mid-scale or budget

UMAXX™ Smartroom TV brings the guest’s living room into your hotel. With this cloud-based hospitality TV platform, the hotels guests can use streaming services just as at home. It creates modern digital experiences for your guests and is compatible with all major hotel TV brands. See list of compatible TVs.
